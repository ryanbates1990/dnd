## Raven Queen Fragment
*Medium Celestial (Memory), Neutral*

**Armor Class:** 17 (ghostly plate)  
**Hit Points:** 135 (18d8 + 54)  
**Speed:** 0 ft., fly 60 ft. (hover)

---

**STR** 8 (-1)  **DEX** 18 (+4)  **CON** 16 (+3)  
**INT** 20 (+5)  **WIS** 18 (+4)  **CHA** 22 (+6)

**Saving Throws:** Wis +8, Cha +10, Con +7  
**Damage Resistances:** necrotic, psychic, radiant, cold, lightning;  
  bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks  
**Damage Immunities:** poison  
**Condition Immunities:** charmed, frightened, paralyzed, exhaustion  
**Senses:** truesight 60 ft., passive Perception 14  
**Languages:** all, telepathy 60 ft.  
**Challenge:** CR 10 (5,900 XP)  
**Proficiency Bonus:** +4

---

### Divine Echo
This is a fractured, echoing memory of the Raven Queen. It exists for 1 minute or until reduced to 0 hit points, after which it dissipates in a burst of black feathers.

---

### Innate Spellcasting (Divine)
The fragment’s innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 18). It can cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

- **At-will:** *Thaumaturgy, Toll the Dead, Sacred Flame* (2d8 radiant)  
- **3/day each:** *Banishment, Silence, Guiding Bolt* (5th level, 8d6 radiant)  
- **1/day each:** *Wall of Light, Spirit Guardians* (radiant, 30 ft radius, 3d8 per round)

---

### Actions

**Radiant Shear** *(Melee or Ranged Spell Attack)*  
+10 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 60 ft., one target.  
Hit: 27 (6d6 + 6) radiant damage.  
Target must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened until the end of their next turn.

**Wail of Unraveling** *(Recharge 5–6)*  
Each enemy within 30 feet must make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw.  
- On a failure: 4d10 psychic damage and knocked prone.  
- On a success: Half damage, no prone.  
Undead automatically fail and take maximum damage.

---

### Legendary Resistance (1/Day)
If the fragment fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

---

### Legendary Actions (2 per round)
The fragment can take 2 legendary actions per round, only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Grasping Wings.** One target within 30 feet must make a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw or be restrained until the end of its next turn.

- **Flicker of Memory.** One creature of the fragment’s choice regains the ability to use a spent reaction.

- **Sever the Tether (Costs 2 Actions).** One undead within 60 feet must succeed on a DC 18 Charisma saving throw or be banished back to death. If its CR is 5 or lower, it vanishes instantly.

---

### Appearance and Flavor
The fragment appears as a pale, beautiful woman of indeterminate age, clad in drifting raven feathers and veils of shadow and starlight. Her face shifts constantly between grief and resolve. Her presence pulls the warmth from the air—but it's not evil, just overwhelming.

*Her voice is a whisper across a battlefield:*  
*"You remember. Then let me remind him."*
