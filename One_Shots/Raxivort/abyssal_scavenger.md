# Abyssal Scavenger
*Medium Fiend (Demon), Chaotic Evil*

___
**Armor Class** 13 (Natural Armor)

**Hit Points** 22 (4d8 + 4)

**Speed** 30 ft., Burrow 10 ft., Climb 20 ft.
___
|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
|14 (+2)|14 (+2)|13 (+1)|3 (-4)|12 (+1)|5 (-3)|

___
**Saving Throws** Con +3

**Skills** Perception +3, Stealth +4

**Damage Resistances** Fire

**Damage Immunities** Poison

**Condition Immunities** Poisoned

**Senses** Blindsight 30 ft. (blind beyond this radius), Tremorsense 30 ft., Passive Perception 13

**Languages** Understands Abyssal but can't speak

**Challenge** 1 (200 XP)

---

## Traits

### Carrion Instinct
The scavenger always knows the direction of the nearest creature below half its hit point maximum within 60 feet.

### Abyssal Physiology
When the scavenger dies, a creature proficient in Medicine may spend 1 minute harvesting intact abyssal organs. A successful DC 12 Medicine check yields **1 Abyssal Organ**. On a failure, the organs are ruined.

### Cowardly Predator
If reduced below half its hit points while no allied scavengers remain within 20 feet, the scavenger must succeed on a DC 12 Wisdom saving throw or use its movement to flee on its next turn.

---

## Actions

### Bite
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.

**Hit:** 6 (1d8 + 2) piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) necrotic damage.

---

### Corpse Drag
The scavenger attempts to grapple one prone or unconscious Medium or smaller creature.

Escape DC 12.

While grappling in this way, the scavenger may move at full speed without reducing its movement.

---

### Fetid Bile (Recharge 5-6)
The scavenger vomits corrosive digestive slime in a 15-foot cone.

Creatures in the area must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw.

- **Failure:** 7 (2d6) acid damage and the creature can't regain hit points until the end of its next turn.
- **Success:** Half damage and no healing restriction.
