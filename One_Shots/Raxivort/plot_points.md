## Crossing Hazards

| **2** | **Rat Sabotage** | Panicked rat workers flee across the scaffolding. One frantically severs a support rope, causing part of the bridge to buckle. | **3** | **Bursting Egg Sac** | A swollen egg sac overhead ruptures, showering the area with writhing larvae. | **4** | **Something Below** | The entire feeding pit ripples outward. A massive shape glides just beneath the surface of the larvae before disappearing into the darkness. Nothing attacks... for now. |
| **5** | **Fungal Spores** | A cloud of sickly yellow spores drifts across the bridge. Creatures must succeed on a **DC 13 Constitution saving throw** or begin coughing violently, reducing their speed by **10 feet** until the end of their next turn. |
